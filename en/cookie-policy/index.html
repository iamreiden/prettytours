Cookie Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

This page explains in full transparency which cookies Pretty Tours uses on its website, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences at any time.

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. It is stored by your browser and allows the website to remember information about you during future visits.

Cookies can be:

  • Session cookies: temporary, deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: stored on your device for a defined period.
  • First-party cookies: placed directly by Pretty Tours.
  • Third-party cookies: placed by external services (e.g. Google Analytics).

Cookies cannot access your personal files, transmit viruses, or directly identify you unless you have voluntarily provided your information.

3. What Pretty Tours does not do

  • We never sell your browsing data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising tracking cookies (Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • We do not place commercial profiling cookies
  • We do not share your analytics data for commercial purposes
  • We do not combine your cookie data with other sources to personally identify you

4. Managing your cookie preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time. By clicking the button below, your saved preferences will be cleared and the consent banner will reappear, allowing you to make a new choice.

Your choices are saved for 12 months. After this period, the banner will reappear automatically.

5. Deleting cookies from your browser

Independently of our tools, you can delete or block cookies directly from your browser settings:

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data → See all cookies and site data → Remove all.

Mozilla Firefox

Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data.

Apple Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All.

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Privacy, search and services → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data.

Please note: Blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites, including ours (language settings, preferences). Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled without impacting the service.

6. International transfers — Google Analytics

Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*) are managed by Google LLC, a US-based company. Data collected via these cookies may be transferred to the United States.

These transfers are governed by:

  • The Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) of the European Commission, adopted by Google
  • The EU–US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), in force since July 2023
  • Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can globally opt out of Google Analytics via the official browser add-on: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

8. Legal basis

In accordance with the Data Protection Act 2017 (DPA 2017) of Mauritius and the guidelines of the Information and Communication Technologies Authority (ICTA):

  • Strictly necessary cookies (pretty_tours_consent, PHPSESSID): exempt from consent — legal basis: legitimate interest and service operation.
  • Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*): subject to your prior, free, informed and specific consent — legal basis: consent (Art. 50 DPA 2017).
