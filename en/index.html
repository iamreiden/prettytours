Discover my story

Who am I?

My name is Pretty, and Mauritius is my entire life.

Born here in 1980, daughter of a taxi-guide, I grew up between the reefs of Mahébourg and the sugar cane fields of Chamarel. My father told me the story of every stone, every tree. By age 6, I knew the lagoon currents better than my multiplication tables.

After years working in luxury hospitality, I realized something: tourists didn't just want to "visit" Mauritius. They wanted to FEEL it. To understand why we say "mo pays" with such pride. Why the séga makes our souls vibrate.

In 2019, I founded Pretty Tours. Not an agency. Not a business. An invitation to discover my island through my eyes.

"With me, you will never be a number in an air-conditioned bus. You will be my guest. I'll share the legends my grandmother whispered to me. I'll take you where the 'official' guides never go."

Mauritius is not just a destination. It's an emotion.
And I want to share it with you.
